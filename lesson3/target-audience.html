Target Audience:

The target audience are residents of Preston, Soda Springs and Fish Haven. The residents of these areas are mostly older people aged 50-90, who have lived in the area their whole life and value Idaho’s farming history. Most are now retired, but were earning an income of around 50,000, Since the demographic is older they need a website that is easy to navigate and with relatively large fonts sizes for easy readability.

Personas:

Blanche Lloyd

Blanche is 89 and lives alone in Soda Springs. She has never owned a desktop, but her grandchildren recently bought her an iphone XR she doesn't know how to use it very well, but really likes to know the weather forecast, so she goes on to It’s Idaho Weather to see the upcoming forecast in her area once a week.

Linda Buttles

Linda is 52 she lives in Preston, her and her husband are recently retired. She likes to check the weather in her area on her ipad every morning as she gets out of bed. Her husband prefers to check the weather on his desktop computer, because he likes to surf the web on the bigger screen.

Scenarios:

Are there any big storms coming through that will delay my children’s flight?

The sunset was really pretty last night I want to find a picture.

Will I need a jacket to watch my grandchild’s t-ball game?

Will it freeze tonight? Do I need to cover my tomatoes?

My joints are acting up is there going to be rain?
